PermaDrop

The Bluebook (Rule 18.2) requires permanent, archived URLs for online sources cited in legal scholarship. PermaDrop automates this: upload a Word document, archive every URL to perma.cc in one click, and download a clean or redline copy — original formatting fully preserved.
Free & open source · Runs entirely in your browser · Nothing leaves your device
